# Higo's Mission, Vision, and Purpose (Foundational)

---
## 0. The Great Question (Our Compass)
The daily work and the reason why Higo advances relentlessly are born from conquering a question that needs an answer:

> *If the business model is the answer to new times of value distribution, if technological wealth is distributed with the aim of sharing a special love and with the responsibility of caring for what truly gives us happiness, then will Higo users support the vision and support local commerce?*

---
## 1. The Fundamental Purpose: Why does Higo exist?

Higo's fundamental purpose stems from falling in love with a deep problem: **the challenge of teaching humanity to care for life**. For its creators, caring for a living being is the ultimate exercise in human growth, a **"gym for the heart"** that demands patience, discipline, and knowledge, and teaches us to love unconditionally.

The philosophy driving everything Higo does is summarized in a clear premise: **learning to share is the only way to grow, and caring for life, in all its forms, is the only way to endure**.

To materialize this purpose, Higo has evolved from being just an application to a **digital ecosystem that unifies technology, commerce, and community**. Higo does what it does by solving real problems on two mutually reinforcing fronts:

**1. For families and pets (Higo V.I.P.):**
Higo seeks to **transform the weight of responsibility into shared joy**. The platform identified that fragmented routines and scattered medical information generate friction and stress in households. Therefore, it designed a tool to **organize love and share care** among family members and caretakers, reducing the stress of pet ownership and turning daily tasks into real opportunities for connection and teamwork.

**2. For local neighborhood shops (Higo Op):**
Higo exists to **return power to traditional commerce**, which currently faces big chains and e-commerce giants at a disadvantage. It does so by providing technological tools to digitize their operations and gain hyperlocal visibility through a fair model **with zero sales commissions**. The goal is to **digitize community trust**, allowing these micro-businesses to thrive while serving their neighborhoods.

Ultimately, Higo's strong vision is to create a circular economy where **commerce finances animal welfare**. Its long-term goal includes democratizing technology (such as lending smart care devices instead of selling them) and creating a total support network. Higo firmly believes that innovation without purpose is meaningless and that **the highest purpose of technology is to protect life**.

## 2. Our Mission
**To democratize access to pet care and welfare**, promoting respect for life and natural balance.

We seek to transform technology into a tool that organizes love and shares responsibility. We alleviate the individual burden of care through community collaboration, ensuring that every pet (regardless of their family's economic context) has access to a support ecosystem, products, and services driven by local commerce.

---
## 3. Our Vision
**To be the definitive and leading digital ecosystem for animal care in the world.**

We visualize a 360º environment (composed of Higo V.I.P. and Higo Op) where the "Multispecies Family" and neighborhood shops thrive together. We want to return power to local commerce against market giants and turn pets into the true financial protagonists of their own well-being. A network where every digital interaction translates into a quality of life improvement in the physical world.

# Bonding as a Strategy: An Anthrozoological Roadmap

Higo's competitive advantage and scientific heart are not based on algorithms, but on **Anthrozoology**: the deep study of the interaction and bond between humans and animals.

### The Multispecies Family
We no longer talk about "owners" and "dogs/cats." We talk about an integrated family nucleus where the companion animal plays a central emotional role. Higo is architecturally designed to reflect, validate, and celebrate this new social structure. Our software recognizes the pet as an individual with their own identity, history, and rights.

### Reducing "Perceived Cost"
Abandonment, abuse, or poor care often arise when the "cost" (financial, time, energy, or emotional stress) exceeds the gratification of the bond.
Higo's anthrozoological strategy attacks this problem directly:
1. **Time/Energy Cost:** Reduced through collaborative care (Family Roles) and shared management of daily and medical tasks.
2. **Financial Cost:** Reduced through the "Higo Economy," where routine purchases generate rewards (Higo Coins) that the pet "owns" to finance their own digital life and benefits.
3. **Emotional Cost:** Reduced through community connection, local commerce support ("Welfare Sources"), and safety protocols (Lost Pet) that eliminate the caregiver's anxiety and loneliness.

### The Definitive Platform
Higo does not compete to sell more products; it competes to **enrich the bond**. By basing our development on anthrozoological principles, we guarantee that every feature (from uploading a photo to ordering delivery) has the ultimate goal of strengthening the human-animal relationship, making care always a celebration and never a burden.

### North Star Metric
Number of "Active Neighborhoods" per week. (An active neighborhood can be defined as a geographical area of 'x' km² that has at least 5 V.I.P. users interacting and 1 Higo Op shop receiving orders).
Higo relies on the PlusCodes Geo-Positional parametrization, which determines that the first 8 characters define a considerable area forming a near-perfect square. For our initial operational case in Colombia, due to its proximity to the equator, we have an area of approximately 270 square meters.
