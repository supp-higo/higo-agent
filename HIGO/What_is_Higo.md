# What is Higo? An Animal Care Revolution

Higo is a digital and technological ecosystem designed as a tool to manage the responsibility of pet ownership, allowing users to share memories, reminders, and medical histories. Through Higo, families and their close community (interest groups) can coordinate their pets' expenses and needs, improving communication. We offer a unique pet profile (public in case of loss) in a friendly learning environment, bringing knowledge closer and creating a support network to obtain products and services.

We envision a world where every pet has a favorite local specialty store, and a favorite dog walker or pet sitter, so that anyone in the world can make a caring gesture towards any animal.
Higo is a space to share responsibility, love, and joy.

## Higo Op - Local, Digital, and Fair Commerce

Our mission is simple yet powerful: **to bring all players in the sector together in a single platform, returning power to local commerce and strengthening the bonds within the pet lovers' community.**

In a market increasingly dominated by large conglomerates and aggressive subscription models that have impacted traditional commerce, Higo emerges as an answer. We are a real opportunity for neighborhood shops, that pillar of trust in our communities, to not only survive but thrive in the digital age.

### The Problem We Solve in Traditional Commerce
Traditional neighborhood pet shops, which have built trust-based relationships over years, face unequal competition. Large retailers and e-commerce giants offer low prices at the cost of aggressive subscription models. Higo was born to level the playing field, offering these traditional businesses an innovative technological tool to digitize their operations and connect directly with the heart of their community.

At the same time, at home, pet care often generates friction due to lack of communication. Higo solves this by becoming the hub of family pet care management with a tool that aims to manage responsibility in an enjoyable and dynamic way.

## How does Higo work?

Higo operates through two interconnected applications that feed into each other:

### 1. Higo Op (Operations) - For Businesses

It is the digital command center for pet shops. Through a fair subscription model **with zero sales commissions**, businesses can:

* **Manage Inventory:** Digitize their product catalog, control stock, and update prices in real-time.
* **Manage Orders:** Receive and manage orders for in-store pickup or home delivery.
* **Access Analytics:** Understand business performance with clear data on sales and popular products.
* **Connect with the Community:** Gain hyperlocal visibility to reach the customers who really matter: their neighbors.

### 2. Higo V.I.P. (Very Important Pets) - Collaborative Care for Your Pet

In daily life, coordinating who feeds the pet, remembering the date of the next vaccine, or simply sharing a joyful moment can become a challenge amidst the schedules and responsibilities of each family member. These minor misalignments, though born out of love, can lead to unintended mistakes, such as a double food portion or a forgotten walk.

Higo V.I.P. was born precisely to solve this: it is a tool designed to optimize family coordination and transform pet care into a collaborative, fun, and organized experience.

#### How does Higo V.I.P. work?

We create a digital space where your pet is the protagonist and the whole family can participate in their well-being:

* **A Unique Profile for Your Pet:** Each pet has its own profile, its digital identity within the Higo ecosystem. This profile is the hub for all their information and the meeting point for everyone who cares for them.
* **A Profile That Accompanies Through Every Stage of Life:** We understand that love for a pet spans their entire life cycle. That's why their profile evolves with them. You can update their status to "Sick" to inform the family about special care, or "Deceased" to honor their memory. This feature allows the community of friends and neighbors to offer support in difficult times, turning Higo into a space to celebrate life and accompany in grief.
* **Collaborative Care with Clear Roles:** To better organize responsibilities, there are several roles:
  * **Owner (Amo Propietario):** The primary person responsible for the pet (maximum 2 per profile).
  * **Family Caretaker (Amo Familiar):** Family members or close caretakers participating in their well-being. This structure ensures everyone knows their role, facilitating communication and daily care.
  * **Follower:** Someone interested in knowing about the pet's well-being and experiences, but who does not live directly with them.
* **Family Memory Album:** A space to treasure every moment. Family members can upload photos and videos, choosing whether they are private (family circle only) or public (to share with the entire Higo community).
* **Community Feed - Discover Neighbors' Pets:** When you share a public photo, it appears on the local community feed. Thanks to geolocation, you can discover and connect with other pets and owners in your area. This feature not only strengthens neighborhood bonds, but is also a vital tool: if your pet gets lost, the community nearby can recognize them and help you find them.
* **A Fair Reward Ecosystem:** Higo V.I.P. introduces an internal economy designed to reward care and participation:
  * **Higo Coins:** Earned by purchasing products or services from the network of local businesses affiliated with Higo. These coins work as credits to unlock V.I.P. features, such as posting photos in the community feed.
  * **Rewards (Bones and Fishes):** The currency of affection. Earned by completing care tasks, interacting with the community, or viewing ads. They function as virtual "likes" or gifts you can give to other pets' photos to show appreciation and identify the most beloved pets in the neighborhood.

## Our Long-Term Vision: A 360° Ecosystem

Higo has begun to connect neighborhood shops with their communities, but this is only the beginning. Our vision is to expand the ecosystem to integrate **all players in the pet market**, creating a 360° solution that includes:

* **Care Professionals:** Dog walkers, trainers, groomers, and pet hotels.
* **Health Services:** Veterinarians and clinics.
* **Logistics and Transportation:** Specialists in pet transport.
* **Industry Players:** Manufacturers, responsible breeders, and animal shelters.
* **Events and Activities:** Calendar of events, fairs, and community activities.

We want Higo to be the single point of reference for any need a pet owner might have, consolidating a fragmented market into a unified, fair, and efficient platform.

**The Higo revolution has begun.**
